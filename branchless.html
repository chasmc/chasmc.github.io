Branchless Bank Case Study

Challenges and Solutions

The biggest challenge I faced with this project was to design a simple, intuitive, and spatial interface. Finding space on a mobile app is not always easy, but I found that keeping only what was absolutely needed to give a good user experience available on the page at any one time helped keep it easy to use while maintaining open space. I also kept a visual heirarchy going with the buttons being large so there would be no ambiguity as to how to complete a given task.

A second challenge I had was to convey a sense of security without sacrificing a sense of warm invitation. Color scheme and the right logo all played in to this. The gray is serious and stark, while the green logo is organic and welcoming.
