Branchless Bank Case Study

Challenges and Solutions

The biggest challenge I faced with this project was to design a simple, intuitive interface without it feeling sterile. I wanted to try and make a banking app a place that the user would enjoy returning to. I achieved this by using an appealing logo and contrasting color scheme combined with a clean, easy to use design.

A second challenge I had was to convey a sense of cold security without sacrificing a sense of warm invitation. Color scheme and the right logo all played in to this. The gray is serious and stark, while the green logo is organic and welcoming.
