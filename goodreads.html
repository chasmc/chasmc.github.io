Challenges and Solutions

The biggest challenge I faced with this project was to design a simple, intuitive interface without it feeling sterile. I wanted to try and make a banking app a place that the user would enjoy returning to. I achieved this by using an appealing logo and contrasting color scheme combined with a user friendly design.

A second challenge I had was to give a sense of security to the user. Color scheme and the right logo all played in to this. I wanted something that balanced between secure and inviting.
