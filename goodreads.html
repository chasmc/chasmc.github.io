GoodreadsCase Study

Challenges and Solutions

I was presented with the challenge of redesigning the bookshelf page on the Goodreads website. The site is very cluttered and unorganized and so I wanted to give more space for the user to breathe in. I also noticed that I wasn't inspired to read when I was on the site. I thought that including larger book cover images would help that.

In the end I cleaned up all of the unnecessary verbage and put the book covers in the limelight.
