Bike MD Case Study

Challenges and Solutions

This project presented me with a few challenges. As the front end developer of a team of three (consisting of a UI designer and a back end developer) the main challenge was to find a way to collaborate and come away with a coherent and functional multi-paged web application. We overcame this challenge by delegating our roles from the get go and helping each other out when needed. Respecting others views while finding common ground can go a long way and we definitely found a way to make that happen with this project.
